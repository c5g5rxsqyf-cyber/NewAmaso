Privacy Policy

Effective date:

This Privacy Policy explains how AMASO (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you visit amaso.nl or contact us via our website.

Note: This is a template and should be reviewed by your legal counsel for your specific business and jurisdiction.

1) Information we collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Contact information: name, email address, company, phone number, country, and the message you submit via the contact form.
  • Technical data: IP address, device/browser details, pages visited, and approximate location derived from IP (if enabled by your tooling).
  • Communications: emails and other communications you send to us and our responses.

2) How we use information

  • To respond to inquiries and provide requested information.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the website.
  • To prevent spam, abuse, and security incidents.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3) Legal bases (EEA/UK)

Where applicable, we process personal data based on:

  • Consent (e.g., where you choose to contact us).
  • Legitimate interests (e.g., securing and improving the site).
  • Contract (where needed to provide services you request).
  • Legal obligations.

4) Cookies and analytics

We may use cookies or similar technologies for basic functionality and, if enabled, analytics. You can control cookies through your browser settings. If you use third-party analytics, you should disclose the provider and include opt-out information.

5) How we share information

We may share information with:

  • Service providers that help us run the website or deliver email (e.g., hosting, email/SMPP providers), under contractual confidentiality obligations where applicable.
  • Authorities if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.
  • Business transfers in connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales.

6) International transfers

If information is transferred outside your country, we take steps to provide appropriate safeguards (e.g., standard contractual clauses) where required by law.

7) Data retention

We retain information only for as long as necessary for the purposes described above, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

8) Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9) Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access, correct, or delete personal data.
  • Object to or restrict processing.
  • Data portability.
  • Withdraw consent (where processing is based on consent).
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@amaso.nl.

10) Children’s privacy

Our website is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
